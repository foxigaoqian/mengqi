Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled the most commonly asked questions from our customers. If you have other inquiries, feel free to contact our technical team at any time

What is the step-by-step process to open an indoor playground?

The general process is: Market Research → Site Selection → Free Custom Design → Contract Sign & Deposit → Production → Shipping → Installation → Grand Opening. We guide you through every step.

Is the playground design really free?

Yes. We provide 100% free custom design services. You just need to provide your floor plan (CAD file preferred), ceiling height, and any specific requirements (budget, theme, preferred games).

How long does it take to get a design?

For a standard indoor playground, the initial 3D design usually takes 2-4 working days. For large-scale or complex theme parks, it may take slightly longer to ensure every detail is perfect.

Can I customize the theme and colors?

Absolutely. We offer fully customized solutions. Whether you want a Space, Jungle, Candy, or Cyberpunk theme, we can tailor the colors and components to match your brand image.

How do I know if a design is good for my business?

A good design isn't just about looks; it's about "Playability" and "Traffic Flow". Our designers focus on high-capacity layouts, avoiding dead corners, and integrating rest areas for parents to ensure maximum profitability.

What safety standards do your products meet?

Safety is our top priority. Our equipment is manufactured strictly according to ASTM (USA) and CE/EN1176 (Europe) standards. Materials are non-toxic, fire-retardant, and eco-friendly.

How long is the production lead time?

Generally, it takes 30-60 days for indoor playgrounds (depending on size) and about 15 days for standard outdoor equipment. We will confirm the exact date in the contract.

Will the final product look exactly like the design?

It will look even better! We conduct a Trial Installation in our factory before delivery to ensure every pipe and connector fits perfectly. We can share photos/videos of this process with you.

What makes your products different from other suppliers?

We focus on "Generation 3.0" play experiences—combining traditional soft play with interactive technology and role-play zones. Plus, we use thicker steel pipes and higher-density foam than the market average for longer durability.

Can I visit your factory?

You are most welcome! We are located in Wenzhou, China. We can arrange airport pickup and hotel booking for you. Seeing our production line is the best way to trust our quality.

Do you ship globally?

Yes, we have exported to over 50 countries including the USA, UK, Australia, Dubai, and Malaysia. We can handle shipping to your nearest port or even directly to your door.

What are your payment terms?

Standard terms are 30% deposit via T/T to start production, and the 70% balance paid before shipment (or against the copy of Bill of Lading).

How much space do I need to start?

It depends on your budget. For a Kids Cafe, 150-300m² is popular. For a Commercial FEC (Family Entertainment Center), we recommend 500m² or more to include diverse attractions like trampolines and party rooms.

How do I calculate my Return on Investment (ROI)?

We can help you with a basic ROI analysis based on your local ticket prices and our equipment capacity. Most of our clients break even within 6-10 months of operation.

Is it difficult to install the equipment?

It depends on the size. For small projects (<150m²), many clients install it themselves using our detailed 3D installation manual and video guides.

Can you send workers to install it for me?

Yes. For large projects (>200m²), we strongly recommend hiring our professional installation team. You would need to cover their travel costs, visa, accommodation, and a daily wage. This ensures the fastest and safest setup.
